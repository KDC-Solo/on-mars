# Card Database (from photos of the physical game, 2026-07-03)

Source photos live in `docs/images/` (local only, gitignored). Cross-checked against the
Reference Book and the Rulebook component list:
24 Blueprint cards · 6 Scientist cards · 12 Earth Contract cards · 16 Private Goal cards ·
9 Mission cards · 12 Solo cards (see `solo-deck.md`) · 12 First Colonist cards.

Status: ✅ photographed & catalogued | ⚠️ partially decoded | ❌ not photographed yet.

## Mission cards (9) ✅

Fully decoded — photo values match the Reference Book text exactly. Format: requirement
count for 2/3/4 players; contributing grants the reward each time until complete.

| Requirement (icon) | 2p | 3p | 4p | Reward |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Research tiles taken from the map | 5 | 5 | 7 | 2 Crystals |
| Blueprints taken from the display | 10 | 10 | 13 | 1 Crystal |
| Advanced Buildings on the map | 8 | 8 | 10 | 1 Crystal |
| Bots placed on the map (initial Bots don't count) | 3 | 4 | 5 | 2 Crystals |
| Discovery tiles taken from the map by Rovers | 5 | 7 | 9 | 1 Crystal |
| Techs taken from the display | 7 | 11 | 12 | 1 Crystal |
| Cubes placed in the Progress area | 8 | 11 | 14 | 1 Crystal |
| Scientists taken from the display | 4 | 4 | 5 | 2 Crystals |
| Earth Contracts completed | 2 | 2 | 3 | 1 Crystal |

Solo relevance: the solo game uses 3 Missions (slots A/B/C); solo cards move the matching
tracker cube from the second deck pass onward, and Lacerda gains the contribution Crystals.

## Blueprint cards (24) ✅ names / ⚠️ details

All 24 photographed. The OP band identifies the level: **±3 OP = Level 1** (upgrades a
Building of any size), **±5 OP = Level 3** (requires a Complex of size ≥ 3) — per the
Reference Book. Bottom icon = resource/building affinity (readings to be verified).

±3 (Level 1): Hydroponic Farm II, Biomarket II, Oxygen Tank II, Private Ship II,
Concentrator II, Wind Turbines II, Automated Production II, Casino II, Gym II,
Metal Deposit II, Construction Yard II, Moisture Vaporator II.

±5 (Level 3): Library II, Biolab II, Command Center II, Aqueduct II, Eco Resort II,
Aerial Elevator II, Radar II, Mineral Mine II, Trade Market II, Research Lab II,
Recycling Bots II, Builder Drone AI600 II.

Per-card ability icons (center medallion) still need decoding — lower priority: the solo
engine needs Blueprint *type/level/scoring*, which is captured; abilities matter to the
player, who reads the physical card.

## First Colonist cards (12: 4 "A" + 8 "B") ✅

The teal cards initially mistaken for the solo deck. Beginner variant ("First Colonists"):

- **A cards (4):** dealt 1 per player, secret. Each shows 4 Building types; when you
  construct one of them, reveal, return to box, draw 2 "B" cards. Quadrant layouts
  (TL/TR/BL/BR): A1 Battery/Water/Plant/Crystal-wheel · A2 Water/Plant/Air/Crystal-wheel ·
  A3 Plant/Air/Battery/Crystal-wheel · A4 Air/Battery/Water/Crystal-wheel.
- **B cards (8):** each a secret task + reward, max 1 completed per turn. Tasks (top icon):
  Learn Tech, travel by Shuttle(?), build Shelter, Hire Scientist, R&D develop, ×2-develop
  variant, place Discovery tile, Obtain Blueprint. Bottom icon = reward (resource/Crystal).

Solo relevance: none directly (variant is for multiplayer first games), but worth data-basing
if the app later adds a "first game" mode.
